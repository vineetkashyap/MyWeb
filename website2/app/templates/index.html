A B O U T U S

SCG that stands for Shreedayini Consulting Group, is a prominent yet rapidly growing consulting firm based in India that applies in depth industry expertise and demanding analysis to assist business leaders and contenders succeed and get practical results with genuine impact. Our uncompromising approach to help clients unfailingly make better decisions, deliver value-added performance in business and create bigger revenue on investment of shareholders.

Since 2015, professional consultants have assembled as SCG from the corners of the India and abroad. Each of them holds specialization, proficiency and focus in their unique area. Yet for all the variety in SCG, every individual of our more than 50 professionals have basic and vital in common at some degree: an objective of facilitating our clients make the most of their business.

At present, we are delighted to work with a few of the potential, innovative and successfully running organizations across the globe. Hence, we look for the logically curious, self-confident and high-achiever types. So, we hire individuals who signifies analysis over hypothesis and respectively our ethos values evidence over belief.

Whatsoever the measure, from our major to our most attentive assignment, we do not take anything for granted. We think through each problem factually based on complete research. And we follow the data without any compromise. We provide not just the orthodox wisdom but also profounder and sharper insights that are considerable and give our clients a data advantage.
